KUGULU PARK



Named after the presence of white and black swans, which are gifts from China and Vienna to Ankara, ,the park today hosts more than 20 bird species.
The park, located between Atatürk Boulevard and Tunalı Hilmi Street, is one of the most famous places in Ankara.
You can easily visit Kuğulu Park by coming to Tunalı Hilmi Street with the buses you take from Kızılay.
